Frequently Asked Questions

What exchange rate will I see?

You will get the worldwide mid-market (interbank) rate, which is accessible around the world. Suppliers frequently include markups or unexpected costs on top of this rate .

Is the exchange rate real-time?

Yes, our currency converter tool uses live, up-to-date exchange rates that are upgraded from the worldwide interbank sources .

Why might bank or provider rates differ?

They regularly incorporate markups or unforeseen expenses. Our converter uses the worldwide mid-market benchmark so you can easily spot and avoid any hidden charges forced by the bank or benefit provider.

How are exchange rates determined?

Exchange rates are driven by its request and supply in the foreign exchange market(forex), which are impacted by worldwide financial information, interest rates, and geopolitical events.

Do rates change often?

Yes the rates fluctuate continually due to market dynamics and patterns like inflation, trade balances, political changes, etc. In this manner, it is always advised to double-check the rates at the exchange time.
